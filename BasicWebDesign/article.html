3 injured as car crashes into local convenience store


On Jan 31st, 2024, at 10:35 AM, Mark Testaberger, age 34, drove his car through the wall of a local convenience store in Ridgefield, Washington.
No fatalities, but three people, including the owner of the store, as well as his two son working in the store, were heavily injured, but are expected to make a full recovery.

Officials say Mr. Testaberger was under the influence of methamphetamine, and reportedly possessed a felony amount of the substance
in his glove compartment. The family of the injured have started a GoFundMe Page to help raise money for their medical treatment and repairs for their store, saying quote:

"We will do whatever we can to keep our family business running"
