Subteams

All of our subteams



Design

The design subteam is responsible for designing a 3D model of the robot
and each part that needs to be machined

Mechanicals

The mechanicals team machines parts and builds the physical
structure of the robot

Controls

The controls subteam is responsible for wiring up the robot and ensuring
that everything has the power it needs

Programming

The programming subteam programs in python and creates the brains of
the robot, allowing it to move in both Teleop and Autonomous mode

Business/Marketing

The B/M team gets the rest of the team the money they need through
sponsors, and finding outreach events for us to participate in
